## Recommendation

**Pivot the MB activation assessment from back_delta to lay_delta now that the bot is LAY-ONLY (MOD-280/281, effective 2026-06-27).** Every prior MB report tracked back prices as the headline parity metric; today's data shows that **soccer same-type lay prices are marginally better on MB** (avg lay_delta −0.022, meaning the layer pays 0.022 less per runner on MB than on BF). This is the relevant metric for the current strategy. Begin tracking lay_delta as the headline figure in each report — in parallel with the existing same-type event count and match rate — so that when soccer same-type volume eventually grows, the lay-execution case for MB activation is already characterised.

This does **not** accelerate activation: golf remains unroutable (the bot's top-10-finish lays have no MB equivalent), the two matcher-integrity gates (Jun 20/21) are prerequisites before trusting any routed price, and soccer same-type volume (~5 thin-league events/day) is far below an actionable threshold. But tracking the right metric from day 1 of the lay-only era means the evidence base will be valid when the volume question is eventually re-examined.
