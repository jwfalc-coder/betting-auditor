## Recommendation

**Confirm the World Cup `MATCH_ODDS` fix is systemic before treating it as activation progress.**

Jun 15 produced the first World Cup same-type match in the series: **Saudi Arabia v Uruguay** matched `one_x_two`↔`MATCH_ODDS` with full runner alignment and MB pricing **+0.20 better on the Saudi Arabia back** (9.00 vs 8.80). But it is **one fixture**. Before drawing any activation conclusion, verify over the next 2–3 days whether other WC group-stage games also match same-type, or whether Saudi/Uruguay was a one-off (e.g. the only WC event MB happened to publish a `one_x_two` market for at scan time). If the pattern holds across multiple WC fixtures with non-negative deltas, soccer World Cup Match Odds becomes the **first credible MB activation candidate** — to be re-rated **Watch** once same-type match rate clears 5%.

Secondary note for the operator (not the recommendation, but adjacent): the new MB `handicap` scanner output has a **line-matching bug** — it pairs handicap selections by runner name while ignoring the handicap line, yielding invalid deltas. Fix this before any Asian Handicap routing is considered.
