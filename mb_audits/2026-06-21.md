## ⚠ Matcher integrity — cross-type handicap mis-match (recurring bug)

No cross-**fixture** false same-type pairs today (unlike Jun 20's Tacuarembo↔Yangpyeong). But the matcher emitted a cross-**type** false pair at near-1.0 confidence:

| MB market | BF market (different product!) | conf | Phantom deltas |
|-----------|-------------------------------|------|----------------|
| `one_x_two` Universidad de Chile vs Santiago Wanderers | `TEAM_B_1` (handicap −1 / +1 line) | 0.947 | back_delta **−1.29** / **+3.12** |

The matcher paired an MB straight 1X2 to a BF **handicap** market because the runner names ("Universidad de Chile", "Santiago Wanderers") align after stripping the `-1`/`+1` line suffix. The deltas are pure nonsense (different products). Notably the analyzer itself skipped this same fixture's BF Asian Handicap as unsupported (`BUG-130` in `last_routine`), yet the MB matcher still produced the pairing. A same-type filter keying only on the **MB** side (`one_x_two`) would NOT catch this — the BF side must also be checked. Excluded from the price table.

This is **untrusted-data behaviour, not injection** — the data is benign; the matching logic over-trusts runner-name similarity across product types.

## Recommendation

**Add a BF-side market-type allowlist to the same-type matcher: an MB `one_x_two`/`money_line` may only pair to BF `MATCH_ODDS` (and MB `outright` only to BF `WINNER`) — reject all other BF product types regardless of runner-name confidence.**

Today the matcher emitted a **conf 0.947** pairing of MB `one_x_two` (Universidad de Chile vs Santiago Wanderers) to BF `TEAM_B_1`, a −1 handicap market — carrying a phantom **+3.12 / −1.29** back_delta from comparing a 1X2 price to a handicap price. This is the **product-type** half of the matcher-integrity problem; Jun 20's recommendation (gate on event-key, not confidence) covered the **fixture** half. Both gates are needed before any MB price-delta routing can be trusted, and both are cheap, deterministic checks. Until they exist, the genuine signal — soccer same-type now showing confirmed mild MB-favorable parity (Bahir Dar +0.20, Dock Sud +0.16, on tight books) — stays unusable, and the same-type filter the brief prescribes will keep admitting cross-type noise. This ranks above match-rate growth: with the WC same-type window closed, matcher correctness, not volume, is the binding constraint on the eventual activation decision.
